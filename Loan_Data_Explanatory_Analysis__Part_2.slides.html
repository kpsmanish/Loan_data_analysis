Explantory part-2¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the Loan interest rate & loan Status changed based on Lister Terms,Loan Amount CurrentCreditLines and proper ratings. What are the factors to define the loan Amount and Which states was buying More loans

Dataset Overview¶

The data consisted of Loan Amount, interest Rate and other attributes of approximately 1,13,937 Loan related records. The Loan data specfically originated after July 2009. twenty five data points were removed from the analysis due to inconsistencies or missing information.
